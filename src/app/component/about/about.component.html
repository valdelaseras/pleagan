What is Pleagan?

A virtuous circle

A better world is a vegan world. At pleagan we believe the most efficient way to working towards this goal is to increase the amount of vegan products on the market. The more vegan products are available, the easier it will be for people to consume more animal free products.

For vegans

Pleagan aims to aggregate the collective pleas and support for vegan products into one platform, so our voices can be heard loud and clear. Our publicly available statistics can help vegans determine which companies and products to support or which ones to avoid.

For companies

Learn about the demands for your vegan product, where demand is concentrated and how you are doing compared to your competitors. Search for relevant pleas here and check out our statistics here.

Don't hesitate to initiate a plea as a business to gauge demand for one of your potential vegan products and adjust your business strategies accordingly.
