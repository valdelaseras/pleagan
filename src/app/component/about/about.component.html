What is Pleagan?

Our purpose

Pleagan aims to aggregate the collective pleas of vegans for plant-based products into one platform, so our voices can be heard loud and clear. Our publicly available statistics help producers to efficiently adjust their business strategies while helping vegans determine which companies and products to support.

A virtuous circle

At pleagan we believe the most effective way to a more vegan world is to increase demand. Consequently, the more plant-based products are available on the market, the easier it will be for people to consume more animal free products.
