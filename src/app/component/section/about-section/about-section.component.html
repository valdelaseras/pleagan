What is Pleagan?

A virtuous circle

We believe one way to working towards a more vegan world is to increase the number of vegan products on the market. The more options available and accessible, the easier for more people to make the shift.

For vegans

While petitions are nothing new, we built Pleagan to focus solely on petitions to veganise existing products.

Pleagan aims to aggregate the collective pleas and support for vegan products into one platform, so our voices can be heard louder and clearer. Our publicly available statistics can help you determine which companies and products to support or which ones to avoid.

For companies

Vegans are a target group like any other on the market.

Learn about demands, where demand is concentrated and how your company is doing compared to your competitors. Search for relevant pleas here and check out our statistics here.
