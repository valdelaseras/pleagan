What is Pleagan?

A virtuous circle

Pleagan is intended to help accelerate the growth of veganism by letting the market do its work.

We think one efficient method to working towards a more vegan world is to increase the number of vegan products on the market. The more vegan products are available and accessible, the easier it will be for more people to consume more animal free products.

For vegans

While petitions are nothing new, we built Pleagan to focus on solely vegan petitions, a platform built and run by vegans.

Pleagan aims to aggregate the collective pleas and support for vegan products into one platform, so our voices can be heard louder and clearer. Our publicly available statistics can help vegans determine which companies and products to support or which ones to avoid.

For companies

Learn about the demands for your vegan product, where demand is concentrated and how you are doing compared to your competitors. Search for relevant pleas here and check out our statistics here.

Don't hesitate to initiate a plea for your own business to gauge demand for one of your potential vegan products and adjust your business strategies accordingly.
