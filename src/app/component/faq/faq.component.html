What happens to my data?

Email

When you support or initiate a plea, you are asked for an email for verification purpose. We verify by email because we don't want ill intended people or bots to spam and diminish the integrity of our pleas.

In the forms you will find an option to receive updates regarding the plea. If you check this box, we will use the email for that purpose. Other than that, your email isn't shared with anybody else. We keep the email in our databases only to prevent users from supporting the same plea with the same email address multiple times.

Location

In the forms we also ask for your country. The purpose of gathering these locations is so the companies know where demand is concentrated. In an ideal world they would do so for a worldwide market. However, they may not always have the resources for this. We want all businesses, large or small, to launch vegan products successfully, so they will happily and naturally invest more efforts into similar products.
