Pleagan explained in 3 simple steps

Plea

Find or initiate a plea to a business to veganise a product of theirs. Gather support from other vegans or show your support for existing pleas.

Inform

With enough support, Pleagan contacts the company in question and informs them of the plea. We keep track of communications and post updates to Pleagan accordingly to our news page.

Profit

When a company answers our pleas and complies they climb the rankings and we promote their brand new vegan product in our Hall of Fame with a permanent spot.
