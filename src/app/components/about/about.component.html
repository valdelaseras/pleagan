What is pleagan?

Pleagan is essentially a platform for two groups:

  • for people on plant-based diets to initiate and support pleas to companies. The goal is a veganised version of an already existing product.
  • for companies to get insight in the vegan market. If handled well, pleagan can be a place for exposure and promotion of new animal free products.

A virtuous circle

Of course many petitions and initiatives already exist scattered over the internet. Pleagan aims to aggregate this into one place, to help vegans to have their collective pleas for more plant-based products heard loud and clear. Businesses can use the information on Pleagan to efficiently adjust their business strategies accordingly.

At pleagan we believe the most effective way to a more vegan world is to increase demand. Consequently, the more plant-based products are available on the market, the easier it will be for people to consume more animal free products. Everybody wins.

How pleagan helps your business

Quick tip: you can initiate a plea yourself to gauge demand for your product.

Stats

With every plea we gather some stats and put them in handy graphs. These stats are intended to give you insight in the demand for the product, where demand might be the highest and how the plea for your product is doing compared to your competitors.

These statistics are freely available for everybody to see. If you search your company or product name here and a plea comes up, open the plea and you will find the stats, along with a list of all supporters of the plea. You can also check out our leaderboards.

If a plea to your company has been initiated and a certain threshold of support has been reached, we will reach out to you.

Exposure

If you respond to our pleas and bring out a new animal free product, we happily offer you and your product exposure in several ways, to keep that snowball effect going:

  • We send out an email to all supporters of the plea who have checked the box to receive a notification
  • Any news relating to your animal free products will be posted on our news page
  • Your product is permanently placed in our Hall of Fame
  • You will climb the rankings of our leaderboard

How to use Pleagan

As mentioned above, the goal is to have businesses either replace a non-vegan product with a vegan one, or at the least add a vegan product to their inventory. You can either initiate a new plea or look through all existing pleas and add your support to it.

Initiating a plea

When you initiate a new plea, we crosscheck the product you entered in the form with all product and company names in our plea database. A notification will be displayed if it looks like a plea for that specific product in combination with the company has already been initiated. Please don't ignore this message and check out those other pleas to double check before you proceed with the plea form.

Remember: one plea backed by all supporters is more impressive and effective than several of the same pleas with the support scattered between those.

You can always do a quick search beforehand in all pleas on either product or company name.

Supporting a plea

This part is pretty straightforward. You don't have to leave a message to companies, you can just show your support by backing the plea with your name.

However, we strongly encourage you to leave a short message to the business to make it more impactful. Let them know why you really want or need their product!
