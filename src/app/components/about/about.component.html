What is Pleagan?

Pleagan aims to aggregate the collective pleas of vegans for more plant-based products into one place, so they can be heard loud and clear. Businesses can then use the information to efficiently adjust their business strategies.

A virtuous circle

Everybody wins

At pleagan we believe the most effective way to a more vegan world is to increase demand. Consequently, the more plant-based products are available on the market, the easier it will be for people to consume more animal free products.
