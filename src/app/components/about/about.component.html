What is pleagan?

Pleagan aims to aggregate the collective pleas of vegans for more plant-based products into one place, so they can be heard loud and clear. Businesses can then use the information to efficiently adjust their business strategies.

A virtuous circle

At pleagan we believe the most effective way to a more vegan world is to increase demand. Consequently, the more plant-based products are available on the market, the easier it will be for people to consume more animal free products. Everybody wins!

How pleagan helps your business

Stats

Find out the demand for your vegan product, where demand is concentrated and how you are doing compared to your competitors. Find the relevant pleas and statistics by searching for your company name here and don't forget to check out our leaderboards.

Quick tip: you can also initiate a plea yourself to gauge demand for your product.

Exposure

If you respond to a plea and bring out a vegan product, we happily offer you and your product exposure in several ways, to keep that snowball effect going:

  • We send out an email to all supporters of the plea who have checked the box to receive a notification
  • Any news relating to your animal free products will be posted on our news page
  • Your product is permanently placed in our Hall of Fame
  • You will climb the rankings of our leaderboard
