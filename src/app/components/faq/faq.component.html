What happens with my data?

Email

When you support or initiate a plea, you are asked for an email for verification purpose. We verify by email because we don't want bots to spam and diminish the integrity of our pleas. Additionally it's an extra safety measure to prevent ill intended people and bots from abusing the platform.

In the forms you will find an option to receive updates regarding the plea. If you check this box, we will use the email for that purpose. Other than that, your email isn't shared with anybody else. We keep the email in our databases only to prevent users from supporting the same plea with the same email address twice ( or more ).

Location

In the forms we also ask for your location. It is up to you to decide how precise you want to be. You can enter the city you live in, the country, state or just the continent you are on. The purpose of gathering locations is so the companies know where demand is concentrated.

In an ideal world, of course, they would do so for a worldwide market. They may not always have the resources for this and we especially want businesses, large or small, to release successful vegan products so they will happily invest more efforts into similar products.

Stay updated

If you check the box to receive updates when initiating or supporting a plea, you will also receive notifications by mail when a target has been reached or when a business has launched the product.

Can I contribute to Pleagan?

Yes!

Will I be paid?

No

It's not because we are stingy, we just don't generate any money with Pleagan ;)

All work on Pleagan has been done on voluntary basis and personal investments. We are however really excited and grateful if anyone is willing to contribute any work to Pleagan. We love the idea of Pleagan being a community project.

Contributions can include all sorts of help, from sending in new ideas, copywrite improvements, contributing visual work, code, you name it. We have a special thanks page dedicated to all those who have made Pleagan possible in some way.

Developers

Pleagan is open source. Please help.

UX, UI and Visual Artists

Besides code contributions, we also love to change up our theme and styles from time to time. Pleagan is built without UX or visual design professionals and we did our best to make it look decent ;). If you are and have some cool ideas for Pleagans, you are most welcome to get in touch with us and have a chat!
